# JunoClaw — Studio Ghibli Art Prompts

> 2D hand-painted style. Old world in raw form integrated with the machine.
> Sovereign family life by the sea. Subtle techie vibe meets Ghibli warmth.
> Think: Nausicaä's world but the technology is blockchain, not airships.

---

## 1. The Seaside Workshop

```
Studio Ghibli 2D hand-painted illustration, warm afternoon light.
A weathered stone cottage perched on a grassy sea cliff, laundry drying on a rope line.
Through the open wooden window: a cluttered workshop desk with a glowing terminal screen
showing scrolling green text (blockchain transactions), surrounded by dried herbs,
copper tools, hand-drawn circuit diagrams pinned to the wall with wooden pegs.
A cat sleeps on a stack of leather-bound notebooks labeled "WAVS" in faded gold.
The sea is turquoise and calm below, fishing boats in the distance.
Wildflowers grow through cracks in the stone wall.
Color palette: warm ochre, sea-glass teal, faded copper, soft cream.
Hand-painted texture, visible brushstrokes, Hayao Miyazaki style.
```

## 2. The Verification Garden

```
Studio Ghibli 2D illustration, golden hour.
A terraced hillside garden overlooking the ocean.
Among the tomato vines and bean poles, small translucent crystal nodes
float at knee height, connected by thin threads of light — a mesh network.
A grandmother in a linen apron tends the garden with one hand
while her other hand rests on a smooth stone pedestal with a softly glowing
hexagonal seal (the DAO governance sigil). A child watches, fascinated.
Butterflies carry tiny luminous data packets between the crystal nodes.
In the background: a weathered lighthouse with a slowly rotating
holographic beacon instead of a lamp. Fishing nets dry on wooden racks.
Color palette: amber gold, garden green, crystal blue, warm brown.
Hand-painted, watercolor edges, cel-shaded shadows.
```

## 3. The Family Table (Governance in Session)

```
Studio Ghibli 2D, evening interior, oil lamp warmth.
A large wooden kitchen table in a seaside farmhouse.
Around the table: a family of five — grandmother, parents, two children —
and a small brass automaton sitting in a child's high chair,
its chest panel displaying a softly pulsing vote tally (green/red bars).
On the table: a shared meal of bread, fish, and vegetables,
alongside a worn leather-bound ledger open to a page titled "Proposal #7"
with hand-written entries and a wax seal. A quill pen rests in an inkwell.
Through the window: stars over a dark sea, and a distant cluster of
floating lanterns (validator nodes) hovering above the water.
The room feels lived-in, warm, sovereign. No corporation. Just family deciding together.
Color palette: lamp amber, wood brown, star blue, brass gold, parchment cream.
Miyazaki domestic interior style, detailed clutter, cozy chaos.
```

## 4. The Attestation Lighthouse

```
Studio Ghibli 2D illustration, dramatic sunset.
A stone lighthouse on a rocky promontory, waves crashing below.
Instead of a glass lamp room, the top houses a slowly rotating
crystalline mechanism — the TEE enclave — casting prismatic light
in structured patterns across the sea (attestation beams).
A young keeper climbs the spiral stairs carrying a bundle of scrolls
(signed transactions). The lighthouse walls are carved with circuit-like
patterns that look ancient, like runes, but are clearly technological.
Seabirds nest in alcoves alongside small blinking sensor modules.
A wooden sign at the base reads "VAIRAGYA STATION" in faded paint.
The sunset paints everything in deep amber and violet.
Color palette: sunset amber, deep violet, stone grey, crystal white, sea foam.
Nausicaä-era Ghibli, epic scale, hand-painted.
```

## 5. The Sovereign Harbor

```
Studio Ghibli 2D panoramic illustration, early morning mist.
A small harbor village built into sea cliffs.
Wooden fishing boats with subtle antenna arrays moored at stone piers.
Market stalls open for the day — one sells vegetables, another sells
hand-carved keyring tokens (physical NFTs). Children run between stalls.
At the center of the harbor square: a mossy stone well with a
holographic display floating above the water showing the DAO treasury
balance and active proposals. Villagers pause to read it, casually,
like checking a community noticeboard.
On the hillside above: terraced gardens with the crystal node mesh
from prompt #2 visible between the rows. Smoke rises from cottage chimneys.
A merchant's boat arrives carrying crates stamped with "AKASH COMPUTE."
Color palette: morning blue-grey, stone warm grey, market colors (orange, red, green),
holographic teal, mist white.
Spirited Away marketplace energy, Porco Rosso seaside.
```

## 6. The Agent at Work

```
Studio Ghibli 2D illustration, quiet night scene.

On the desk: a small brass-and-wood automaton (the AI agent) sits cross-legged,

Through the window: the attestation lighthouse beam sweeps across the dark water.
Color palette: moonlight silver-blue, brass amber, paper cream, night indigo.
Intimate Ghibli interior, Kiki's Delivery Service bedroom warmth.
```

## 7. The Budding Ceremony

```
Studio Ghibli 2D illustration, ceremonial dusk.
Thirteen figures stand in a circle on a grassy headland above the sea.
In the center: a ancient stone tablet with the genesis address carved into it,
glowing faintly. From the tablet, thirteen thin roots of light
extend outward to each figure's feet (the budding — weight distribution).
Each figure holds a different tool: a fishing net, a compass, a ledger,
a telescope, a hammer, a brush, a lantern, a compass, a seed bag,
an automaton companion, a scroll, a crystal, a key.
The central figure (Genesis) is stepping back, hands open, releasing control.
Behind them: the lighthouse beam sweeps. Stars emerge. The sea is calm.
Color palette: ceremony gold, twilight purple, grass green, stone grey, starlight.
Princess Mononoke ceremonial energy, Ghibli epic composition.
```

## 8. The Swap (Junoswap in Action)

```
Studio Ghibli 2D illustration, bustling market noon.
Two merchants at a harbor-side stall exchange goods:
one hands over a basket of blue crystals (JUNO tokens),
the other returns a stack of copper discs (USDC).
Between them on the counter: a small mechanical balance scale
with crystalline weights that glow when the exchange is fair.
Above the scale, a tiny holographic checkmark appears (WAVS attestation).
A crowd browses nearby stalls. A child points at the glowing checkmark, amazed.
The stall's wooden sign reads "JUNOSWAP v2" in hand-painted letters
with a small "verified ✓" stamp burned into the wood.
Color palette: noon bright, market warm (terracotta, saffron, jade),
crystal blue, copper, holographic green.
Spirited Away bathhouse marketplace energy.
```

- **Midjourney**: Prefix with `--ar 16:9 --style raw --v 6.1` for cinematic ratio
- **DALL-E 3**: Use as-is, add "in the style of Studio Ghibli, 2D hand-painted animation cel"
- **Stable Diffusion**: Add `ghibli style, anime, hand-painted, watercolor, cel shading` to positive prompt
- These prompts work for: Medium article headers, governance proposal banners, social media, pitch decks
- **Do not** use for anything claiming official Ghibli affiliation
