NOTE TO FUTURE SELF:
The discipline is: ship the safe failure mode first, then iterate. The v30
RetentionWindowHeights = 0 default is the canonical example - unbounded
storage growth is the better failure mode than silently zeroing sparse
delegators. The same shape applies to our LST allowlist (empty by default
at junoclaw launch) and our agent-registry's default reputation (zero, not
implicit-positive).
